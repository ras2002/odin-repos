TEA!


Refreshing tea in the evening

Ingredients & Tools required

  • little water
  • milk
  • sugar
  • tea powder

The process!

  1. In a vessel take a little amount of water and boil it for 5 minutes
  2. now add tea powder into the boiled water
  3. after some time add few cubes of sugar as per the requirement
  4. at the end add milk
  5. allow the milk also to boil and atlast it can be severed into the cups eventually

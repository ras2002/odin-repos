Amazing bhel puri

Bhel puri is an amazing snack for kids .

Now-a-days most of them can have it as it can be prepared easily anywhere at home .

Ingredients & Tools required

  • Sev
  • Potato
  • Puffed Rice
  • Onion
  • Tamrind chutney
  • Chilli pepper
  • Tomato
  • chat masala
  • lemon
  • chilli powder and peanuts

The process!

  1. 1 cup (25 g) of puffed rice (mumura).
  2. 1 tablespoon (10 g) of chopped red onion
  3. 1/2 teaspoon (2.5 g) of salt
  4. 1/2 of a green chili
  5. Zest of 1/2 a lemon
  6. 10 roasted, shelled peanuts
  7. 2 tablespoons (19 g) of bhel sev
  8. 4 puris, broken 2 whole puris
  9. A squeeze of fresh lemon juice
